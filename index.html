Format

  • BGIS 2024 - The Grind: February 5th - 23rd, 2025
    • 64 Invited Teams, 16 matchdays.
    • Teams divided into 4 groups with 16 squads in each group.
    • Each group will play 24 matches in respective weeks.
    • All 64 teams based on the overall standings will seed to BGIS 2025: Main Event
      • Round 1: Feb 16th - 23rd, 2025
        • 1024 Teams, 8 matchdays.
          • 1016 Teams from In-Game Qualifiers.
          • Bottom 8 Teams from The Grind Week-1.
        • Teams divided into 64 groups with 16 Teams in each group.
        • Each team plays a total of 6 matches.
        • Top 7 Teams from each group + Top 48 Teams among the remaining teams (Total 496 Teams) qualify for Round 2.
      • Round 2: Feb 27th - Mar 2nd, 2025
        • 512 Teams, 4 matchdays.
          • 496 Teams from Round 1.
          • Top 8 Teams from The Grind Week-1.
          • Bottom 8 Teams from The Grind Week-2.
        • Teams are divided into 32 groups with 16 Teams in each group.
        • Each team plays a total of 6 matches.
        • Top 7 Teams from each group + Top 16 Teams among the remaining teams (Total 240 Teams) qualify for Round 3.
      • Round 3: Mar 6th - 9th, 2025
        • 256 Teams, 4 matchdays.
          • 240 Teams from Round 2.
          • Top 8 Teams from The Grind Week-2.
          • Bottom 8 Teams from The Grind Week-3.
        • Teams are divided into 16 groups with 16 Teams in each group.
        • Each team plays a total of 6 matches.
        • Top 6 Teams from each group + Top 16 Teams among the remaining teams (Total 112 Teams) qualify for Round 4.
      • Round 4: Mar 13th - 16th, 2025
        • 128 Teams, 4 matchdays.
          • 112 Teams from Round 3.
          • Top 8 Teams from The Grind Week-3.
          • Bottom 8 Teams from The Grind Week-4.
        • Teams are divided into 8 groups with 16 Teams in each group.
        • Each team plays a total of 6 matches.
        • Top 7 Teams from each group (Total 56 Teams) advance to Quarter Finals.
        • Top 16 Teams among the remaining teams advance to Wildcard.
      • Quarter Finals: Mar 20th - 23rd, 2025
        • 64 Teams, 4 matchdays.
          • 56 Teams from Round 4.
          • Top 8 Teams from The Grind Week-4.
        • Teams are divided into 4 groups with 16 Teams in each group.
        • Each team plays a total of 6 matches.
        • Top 16 Teams based on the cumulative overall standings advance to the Semi Finals Week 1.
        • Remaining 48 Teams advance to the Wildcard.
      • Wildcard: Mar 24th - 27th, 2025
        • 64 Teams, 4 matchdays.
          • 48 Teams from Quarter Finals.
          • 16 Teams from Round 4.
        • Teams are divided into 4 groups with 16 Teams in each group.
        • Each team plays 3 matches over 2 days.
        • After 2 days, based on the overall standings teams are re-shuffled.
        • Again, each team plays 3 matches for 2 days.
        • Top 16 Teams based on the cumulative overall standings advance to the Semi Finals Week 1.
      • Semi Finals Week 1: Mar 29th - Apr 1st, 2025
        • 32 Teams, 4 matchdays.
          • 16 Teams from Quarter Finals.
          • 16 Teams from Wildcard.
        • Teams are divided into 4 groups with 8 Teams in each group.
        • Round-robin format.
        • Each team plays a total of 12 matches.
        • Top 8 Teams advance to the Grand Finals.
        • Remaining 24 Teams proceed to Semi Finals Week 2.
      • Semi Finals Week 2: Apr 3rd - 6th, 2025
        • 24 Teams, 4 matchdays.
        • Teams are divided equally into 3 groups of 8.
        • Double Round-robin format.
        • Each team plays a total of 16 matches.
        • Top 8 Teams advance to the Grand Finals.
    • The Grand Finals: Apr 25th - 27th, 2025 at TBD Kolkata.
      • 16 Teams, 3 matchdays.
        • 8 teams from Semi Finals Week 1.
        • 8 teams from Semi Finals Week 2.
      • 18 matches (6 each day).
